Ongoing Projects

Shear-Flow Stabilized Z-Pinches

Fusion energy requires high energy plasmas, but confining and stabilizing plasma is notoriously difficult. One longstanding proposal is to use shear flow to stabilize Z-pinches. I'm currently simulating Shear-Flow Stabilized (SFS) Z-Pinches using the Gorgon MHD Code developed by Professor Jerry Chittenden of Imperial College London. We are using a radial wire array to generate shear flow over the surface of an axial target wire.

Fire Alarm Clock

Two years ago, when Cornell was renovating the Baker 200 Lecture Hall, I collected a disposed fire alarm and its "pull down" handle. I am now designing an "fire alarm clock" where the handle is pulled to turn off the alarm! My plan is to use an arduino, a DS3231 Real-Time Clock, and an OLED display.
